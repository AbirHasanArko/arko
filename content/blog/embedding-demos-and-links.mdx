## Links, internal and external

Plain `[text](url)` still works — but external links now open in a new tab with a small ↗ glyph, and internal links route through Next.js for fast navigation. See the [home page](/) or my [GitHub](https://github.com/AbirHasanArko) for examples.

## Embedded YouTube

Drop a YouTube embed URL straight into the post:

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/dQw4w9WgXcQ" title="Demo video" />

The wrapper preserves 16:9 aspect ratio, lazy-loads the iframe, and only grants sensible permissions.

## A live Hugging Face Space

For AI projects I often embed the running space so readers can poke at it without leaving the post. Use a taller `height` for app-style demos:

<iframe src="https://arko-hasan-ai-function-validator.hf.space" title="AI Function Validator (live)" height="640" />

## Smaller, fixed-height embeds

<iframe src="https://codepen.io/team/codepen/embed/preview/PNaGbb" title="CodePen demo" height="360" />

That's the whole API — write MDX as usual, and the custom `a` and `iframe` components take it from there.
